Lutrix Technologies is a 100% Batswana youth owned digital media & web development utility company established in 2018. Our goal is to revamp the business landscape in Botswana by taking businesses to the next level and unlocking hidden gems.

The future is online and it is here. So have a piece of the pie and go digital with us! We will go on a journey of self discovery, from the basic branding (your company logo and business cards design), website and application building to (but certainly not limited to) online advertising for your company because we understand how passionate you are about what you do.

Any business can become an established brand, it just needs the right partners to achieve this. Explore our services, try out our products and give us a call when you are ready to blossom!
